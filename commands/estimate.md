# /aria:estimate - Cost & Timeline Estimation

## Purpose

Provide regulatory project cost and timeline estimation framework.

## Status

⚠️ **Under Development**

This command is planned for future release.

## Planned Features

- Cost estimation (optimistic/expected/pessimistic)
- Timeline estimation with milestones
- Consulting, testing, and regulatory fees
- Notified Body costs (EU)
- Cost breakdown by category
